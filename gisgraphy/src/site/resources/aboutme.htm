About me

Hi all, my name is David Masclet, i am developer and i really like GIS and all the stuff around it.

I have started gisgraphy on november 2007, the initial goal of the project was to offer a free, opensource and esay way to use Geonames data.

Gisgraphy needs you, please give us some , on the services, things you've appreciate, functionnalites you wish to have on the next release, problems you've got, bugs you 've found, ... Step by step, i have build the framework, first an importer with an IHM, then a geolocalisation and a fulltext webservice, a back officewith statistics, ...

Then i thought that an opensource geocoder can be useful, so i extends the framework with an importer for Openstreetmap and develop a webservice too.

My goal was to :

  • provide and support a lot of languages (XML, json, PHP, ruby, python, Atom, RSS / GeoRSS , more to come)
  • A high quality software with High test coverage
  • Easy to install, (a minimum to configure to configure)
  • fully internationalize, with a lot of language supported
  • provide an Opensearch module


I hope it is the case. I am currently the only developer.
I also have a blog (in french) where i put some tips and tutorials. You can send me a mail or give some feedbacks, on the services, things you've appreciate, functionnalites you wish to have on the next release, problems you've got, bugs you 've found, ...
